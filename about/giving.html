Support Our Research

Your support can contribute to our continued research and development in the field of robotics and sensorimotor control. By donating, you are contributing to groundbreaking research that aims to improve the quality of life for individuals living with physical disability.

  • Online Donations: Visit our secure donation page to make a contribution online.

    • Why Your Support Matters

    Your generosity enables us to:

    • Advance our research in understanding how we feel and interact with our surroundings.
    • Develop new technologies to aid individuals living with physical disability.
    • Provide unique, immersive educational opportunities for students and researchers.
    • Expand our collaborative efforts with other institutions and researchers.

    Thank You

    We deeply appreciate your interest and support in our mission. Together, we can make significant strides in understanding and improving how we feel and stay in touch.
